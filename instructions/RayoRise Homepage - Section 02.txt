RayoRise Homepage - Section 02: Who We Build For (Development Deliverable)
Objective
This section should immediately help qualified B2B visitors identify themselves and understand that RayoRise manufactures for businesses-not individual consumers.

Within a few seconds, visitors should feel:

"This manufacturer works with businesses like mine."

The goal is to improve buyer confidence, qualify leads, reduce bounce rate, and strengthen SEO and AI discoverability.


Placement
Place this section directly below the Hero.

Order: 1. Hero 2. Who We Build For 3. Company Introduction (About)


Section Heading
Use:

• Small eyebrow heading: WHO WE BUILD FOR
• H2: Manufacturing for Ambitious Apparel Businesses

Add a short paragraph explaining that RayoRise partners with businesses of all sizes-from startups launching their first collection to established brands scaling production-while specializing in football- inspired tracksuits.


Layout
Desktop: 4-column grid (8 cards)

Tablet: 2 columns

Mobile: 1 column

All cards should maintain equal height.
