RayoRise Homepage - New Section: Frequently Asked Questions (Development Deliverable)
Objective
This section should be more than a basic FAQ list.

Its purpose is to answer the most common questions B2B buyers have before requesting a quotation, helping them understand RayoRise's manufacturing process, customization options, production capabilities, and commercial policies.

The goal is to reduce buyer uncertainty, improve trust, and increase conversions while supporting SEO and AI search visibility.


Placement
Place this section directly after the Manufacturing Knowledge Centre and before the Final CTA. Homepage Order:
1. Hero
2. Who We Build For
3. About RayoRise
4. What We Make

6. How It Works
7. What This Means For Your Order
8. Manufacturing Quality

11. Frequently Asked Questions
12. Final CTA
13. Footer


New Section
This is a completely new homepage section. Follow the existing RayoRise design language:
• Premium dark theme
• Gold accent styling
• Consistent typography
• Spacious layout

• Professional accordion design


Section Heading
Use:

• Small eyebrow heading: FAQ
• H2: Frequently Asked Questions

Add a short introduction explaining that this section answers common questions about private-label sportswear manufacturing, customization, product development, sampling, and bulk production.


FAQ Layout
Display FAQs in an accordion layout. Layout:
• Desktop: Two-column accordion (if space allows)
• Tablet: Single column
• Mobile: Single column

Only one accordion item should remain open at a time.


Accordion Behaviour
Each FAQ should display:

• Question
      • Expand (+) icon When opened:
• Show the full answer
• Change plus icon to minus
• Highlight the active item with a subtle gold border
    • Use a smooth expand/collapse animation Keep interactions clean and lightweight.

Initial FAQ Topics
Launch the section with approximately 12-20 useful B2B FAQs, including topics such as:

• What products does RayoRise manufacture?

• What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
• Can I manufacture under my own brand?
• Do you provide samples before bulk production?
• Which branding methods are available?
• Which fabrics can I choose?
• What customization options are available?
• How long does production take?
• Do you ship internationally?
• How do you maintain product quality?
• What information is required before requesting a quote?
• What is the difference between OEM and Private Label manufacturing?

Keep the FAQ list easy to expand in the future.


Answer Style
Every answer should:

• Start with a direct answer
• Briefly explain the process
      • Add helpful context where needed Avoid short replies like:
• Yes
• No
• Contact us

The goal is to educate buyers and build confidence.


UX & Interaction
The FAQ section should help buyers quickly find answers without leaving the page.

It should reduce repetitive pre-sales questions while keeping the homepage clean and easy to scan. Use subtle hover effects and smooth animations only.

Accessibility
Ensure:

• Keyboard-accessible accordion
• Proper ARIA attributes
• Logical heading hierarchy
• Screen-reader friendly labels
• Visible focus states
• WCAG AA color contrast


Performance

• Keep accordion functionality lightweight

• Prefer CSS-based animations
• Render all FAQ content server-side
• Avoid unnecessary JavaScript
• Maintain excellent Core Web Vitals


Future Scalability
Build this section as a reusable CMS-driven component. It should easily support future features such as:
• Unlimited FAQs
• Category-based FAQs
• FAQ search
• Related Knowledge Centre articles
• Product-specific FAQs
• Video answers
    • Multi-language support without changing the overall layout.

Development Notes

• Build reusable accordion components.
• Keep all questions and answers editable through a CMS or JSON.
• Automatically generate FAQPage Schema.
• Allow internal links inside answers where relevant.
• Preserve the existing RayoRise design language.
• Ensure responsive layouts across desktop, tablet, and mobile.


Expected Result
This section should answer buyers' most common manufacturing questions before they contact the sales team, reduce pre-sales friction, improve trust, strengthen SEO and AI visibility, and position RayoRise as a knowledgeable and transparent private-label manufacturing partner.
